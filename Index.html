Protect Your Digital World

Cyber security helps protect systems, networks and data from digital attacks. Learn the basics and stay safe online.

Common Cyber Threats

Phishing

Fake emails or messages used to steal personal information.

Malware

Malicious software designed to damage or access your system.

Ransomware

Attackers lock your files and demand payment to unlock them.

How To Stay Safe

Strong Passwords

Use long and unique passwords for every account.

2FA Security

Enable two-factor authentication whenever possible.

Update Systems

Always keep your software and apps updated.
